## PS4 button

* L1 - manual去auto
* R1 - auto去manual

* UP - semi auto step1 開夾
* LEFT - semi auto step2 放上炮台
* DOWN - semi auto step3 落夾 prepare to fire


* RIGHT - full auto 夾屎

* TRIANGLE - Crossbow Trigger
* CIRCLE - Fire
* SQUARE - Homing
* CROSS - Charge
